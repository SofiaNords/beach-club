News & Events

Spring cleaning, Saturday, May 11

On Saturday, May 11th at 10 AM, we will gather down at the beach to help each other with the annual spring cleaning. We will put in the bathing ladders and lay out the bath mats. We will also take the opportunity to put the raft in the water. Please bring a rake as there is a lot of seaweed to rake up. Everyone can help with something, big or small. We will end with a communal coffee break. Welcome!


Midsummer celebration, Friday, June 21

On Friday, June 21st, we will traditionally celebrate Midsummer on the beach. The celebration starts at 3 PM with dancing around the Midsummer pole. Musicians will sing and play. After that, there will be a lottery and a fish pond. As usual, we will help each other dress the Midsummer pole the night before, on Thursday, June 20th at 6 PM. Contact us if you want to help with other preparations such as the lottery or the fish pond. Warmly welcome!
