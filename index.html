About Tjuvkil Beach

A bathing association

Tjuvkil Beach is a non-profit organization that works to maintain the bathing areas in the Tjuvkil area. The association is responsible for keeping the beach, the pier and the ladders in good condition, as well as organizing social events for the members. The association welcomes anyone who wants to join and support the preservation of the beautiful natural environment in Tjuvkil.
